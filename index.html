ColorizeMe

Bringing Color to the Past.

'm excited to share another project I completed with my team during our bachelor's studies – a website that colorizes black-and-white images, breathing new life into vintage photographs.

Restore memories: By adding color to black-and-white photos, we’re able to enhance the emotional connection to these memories, helping people see the past in a new light.

AI Technology: The project involved training and utilizing AI to analyze and add realistic colors to grayscale images, blending technical skills with creativity.

This project taught us the value of combining innovation with nostalgia, allowing us to preserve and celebrate history through technology.

Psychological Diary

A Digital Space for Mental Wellness

I completed this website along with my group members as a project for my third year in bachelor's. This website was born from a shared vision to create a safe digital space where users can maintain a personal diary, connect with mental health professionals, and schedule counseling sessions.

With this website we aim to:

-Encourage Self-Reflection:

Users can write down their thoughts and feelings in a personal, secure diary.

-Offer Professional Support:

We provide a way for users to easily connect with psychologists and schedule meetings, helping bridge the gap between self-care and professional mental health support.

This project is our small step toward promoting mental health and wellness, empowering individuals to prioritize their mental well-being.
